The idea

This isn't flat-pack. It's the opposite.

Tool-free assembly is only possible because every joint is precision-cut and hand-finished to fit exactly. Easy to build because it's been made properly. Not because it's been made cheap.

The joint

Held by geometry.

Two hook keys thread through a slotted batten and lock the panel solid. Tap them home with a mallet. Tap them out and the piece packs flat.

The material

PaperRock.

A dense composite of compressed paper. Perfectly matte, warm to the touch, solid colour all the way through. Every machined edge reveals a dark layered core, so the joinery reads in every colourway.

  • Light Concrete
  • Dark Concrete
